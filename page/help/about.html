About the Course

UCCP

  • University of California College Prep publishes free high-quality online courses and content to benefit California students, with a special emphasis on helping underserved students gain college eligibility.
  • All UCCP courses are UC-approved. The Advanced Placement® and college prep courses are freely available to California students, teachers and schools.
  • All UCCP courses, including AP Computer Science are aligned to California content standards and are College Board certified.
  • To learn more, visit the UCCP web site, review the Courses, explore the supporting Tools, and see how University of California online courses can provide solutions to California educational challenges.

WISE 4.0 Technology

  • This course is powered by an open-source web 2.0 technology infrastructure called WISE (v4.0) developed under grant from the National Science Foundation.
  • For more information about WISE see its educational website.

Technical Requirements

  • This UCCP course is best viewed using the Firefox browser.
